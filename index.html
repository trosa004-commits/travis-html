Planes are the machines that allow people travel through the sky. They have wings that help lift them into the air when they move fast along a runway. Most planes use engines to push them forward, and a pilot controls where the plane goes. Planes can carry people, food, and other important items to places all over the world. They help people travel long distances much faster than cars or trains.
